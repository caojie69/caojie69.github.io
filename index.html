About Me

I am a professor and also the director of Jiangsu Provincial Key Laboratory of E-Business and National Center for International Joint Research on E-business Information Processing in Nanjing University of Finance and Economics, Nanjing, China. I was selected in the Program for New Century Excellent Talents in University and awarded with the Young and Mid-Aged Expert with Outstanding Contribution in Jiangsu Province. My recent research focus is to employ the advanced methods in the fields of data science, fuzzy system and artificial intelligence, to solve the hot application problems, such as big data analytics, e-commerce, power system and smart finance.

Research Interests

Data Mining, Business Intelligence, Complex Network, Social Computing, Recommendation System
