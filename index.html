I currently working at the College of Information Engineering (Dean of College), Director of Jiangsu Provincial Key Laboratory of E-Business and National Center for International Joint Research on E-business Information Processing in Nanjing University of Finance and Economics, Nanjing, China. My research interests include data mining, fuzzy system, social computing and complex network. My research group attempts to employ the advanced methods in the fields of data science, fuzzy system and artificial intelligence, to solve the hot application problems, such as big data analytics, e-commerce, power system and smart finance.
